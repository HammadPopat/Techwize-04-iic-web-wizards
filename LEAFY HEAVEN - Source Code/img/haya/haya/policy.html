Do's for plants:

Unleash the Power of the Do's and Watch Your Plants Flourish.
  • Do water your plants regularly, but avoid overwatering.
  • Do provide adequate sunlight or shade based on the plant's needs.
  • Do fertilize your plants to provide essential nutrients.
  • Do prune your plants to promote healthy growth.
  • Do repot your plants when they outgrow their containers.

Don'ts for plants:

Avoid the Don'ts and Keep Your Plants Happy and Healthy.
  • Don't overwater your plants, as it can lead to root rot.
  • Don't expose your plants to extreme temperatures or drafts.
  • Don't forget to check for pests and diseases regularly.
  • Don't use excessive amounts of fertilizer, as it can harm your plants
  • Don't neglect your plants' needs for proper care and attention.
